Business Case

Problem: Enterprise AI is locked into single providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). This creates vendor lock-in, data sovereignty risks, and unpredictable costs. 78% of enterprises now use 3+ LLM providers but lack unified orchestration.

Solution: Punky ‚Äî a self-hosted, provider-agnostic autonomous agent platform. Data stays on customer infrastructure. Smart Router sends 70-90% of requests through free tiers. 20 Guardian agents provide autonomous governance. 10+ vertical agent teams (ESG, ERP, HR, PM, Research, OSINT, HSE, Education) deliver industry-specific value.

Moat: Structural differentiators that cannot be copied by feature: self-hosted architecture, provider agnosticism, cost-optimised routing, culturally adaptive Inside Out interface. Competitors would need to rebuild from scratch ‚Äî their business models depend on lock-in.
